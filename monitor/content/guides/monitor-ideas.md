# Monitor Ideas — What Can You Watch?

A reference list of things worth monitoring. Use these as starting points and adapt thresholds to your own situation.

---

## Financial

### Cryptocurrency
- **Bitcoin (BTC)** — Alert if below $70K or above $80K
- **Ethereum (ETH)** — Alert if below $3,000 or above $4,000
- **BNB** — Alert if above $650 (Binance exchange health indicator)
- **Any altcoin you hold** — Alert on ±10% moves

### Stocks
- **Your portfolio holdings** — Alert on ±5% single-day moves
- **Bursa KLCI index** — Alert if below 1,450 (support) or above 1,600 (resistance)
- **Maybank (1155)** — Alert if below RM 9.00
- **Petronas Chemicals (5183)** — Alert on ±3% moves
- **US tech** — Apple, Nvidia, Microsoft on earnings weeks

### Commodities & Rates
- **Gold (XAU/USD)** — Alert if above $2,500 or below $2,200
- **Palm Oil (CPO)** — Alert if below RM 3,500/ton or above RM 4,500/ton (affects plantation stocks)
- **Crude Oil (Brent)** — Alert if above $90 (fuel cost impact)
- **USD/MYR** — Alert if above 4.55 or below 4.35
- **SGD/MYR** — Alert if above 3.55 (Singapore travel/business)
- **USD/EUR** — Alert on ±1% daily moves

## Business

### Your Own Infrastructure
- **Website uptime** — Alert if down for more than 2 minutes
- **API response time** — Alert if average exceeds 1,500ms
- **SSL certificate** — Alert if expiring within 30 days
- **Domain registration** — Alert if expiring within 60 days
- **Server error rate** — Alert if 5xx errors exceed 1% of requests

### Competitive Intelligence
- **Competitor pricing pages** — Alert when page content changes (price updates, new offers)
- **Competitor job postings** — Alert when they post engineering or product roles (growth signal)
- **Competitor product launches** — Alert when their blog/news page updates
- **Your Google Business reviews** — Alert when new review appears

### Market Intelligence
- **Industry news keywords** — "fintech Malaysia", "e-commerce SEA", your industry terms
- **Regulatory updates** — "Bank Negara announcement", "SSM ruling", "Customs Act"
- **Government tenders** — Search ePerolehan for your service category

## Tech & Dev

### APIs & Services
- **Third-party APIs you depend on** — Stripe, Twilio, payment gateways — alert on status page changes
- **Your own API health** — response time, error rate, availability
- **Database connection pool** — Alert on high utilization
- **Queue depth** — Alert if message queue exceeds threshold

### Security & Compliance
- **SSL certificate expiry** — 30 and 7 day warnings
- **Domain expiry** — 60 and 14 day warnings
- **Security advisories** — Alert on CVEs for packages you use (e.g., "npm [package name] vulnerability")
- **Unauthorized DNS changes** — Alert if DNS records change unexpectedly

## News & Keywords

### Brand Monitoring
- **Your brand name** — "aman AI", "aman asmuei", or any branded term
- **Your product names** — alert when mentioned in tech media
- **Founder name** — monitor for mentions in press
- **Your company** — alert on any news article referencing you

### Industry & Macro
- **"Bank Negara" + interest rate** — Malaysian monetary policy moves
- **"Bursa suspension"** — Trading halts on your watchlist
- **"Palm oil ban"** — EU/US import restrictions (affects Malaysian planters)
- **"Ringgit"** — Major MYR news (depreciation, policy)
- **Your competitor names** — Track their PR activity

### Personal Interest
- **Niche keywords** — Topics you want to stay current on
- **Academic papers** — New publications in your research area
- **Conference announcements** — Events in your industry
