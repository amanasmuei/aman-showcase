# Invoice Template

## Structure

```
INVOICE

From:                           Invoice #: INV-[XXX]
[Your Name]                     Date: [Date]
[Your Address/Company]          Due Date: [Date + payment terms]
[Your Email]

To:
[Client Name]
[Client Company]
[Client Address/Email]

─────────────────────────────────────────────

Description                     Amount
─────────────────────────────────────────────
[Project/Service Name]
- [Detail line 1]               [Amount]
- [Detail line 2]               [Amount]
- [Detail line 3]               [Amount]

─────────────────────────────────────────────
                    Subtotal:   [Amount]
                    Tax (X%):   [Amount]
                    TOTAL:      [Amount]
─────────────────────────────────────────────

Payment Terms: Net [30]
Payment Method: [Bank Transfer / PayPal / Wise]

Bank Details:
  Bank: [Bank Name]
  Account: [Account Number]
  Reference: INV-[XXX]

─────────────────────────────────────────────
Thank you for your business!
```

## Invoice Numbering
- Sequential: INV-001, INV-002, INV-003...
- Or by year: INV-2026-001, INV-2026-002...
- Never reuse an invoice number

## Payment Terms Guide
- Net 15: Small projects, trusted clients
- Net 30: Standard for most work
- Net 45: Enterprise/corporate clients only
- Due on receipt: Rush work or very small amounts
