# Lesson 7: Truth, Soul, and the Inner World

## Words

- حَقّ (haqq) → truth / right / due / reality — appears ~287 times
  Ayah: "وَقُلِ الْحَقُّ مِن رَّبِّكُمْ" — "And say: The truth is from your Lord." (18:29)

- نَفْس (nafs) → soul / self / person / breath — appears ~298 times
  Ayah: "كُلُّ نَفْسٍ بِمَا كَسَبَتْ رَهِينَةٌ" — "Every soul is pledged for what it has earned." (74:38)

- سَبِيل (sabil) → way / path / road / cause — appears ~176 times
  Ayah: "قُلْ هَٰذِهِ سَبِيلِي أَدْعُو إِلَى اللَّهِ" — "Say: This is my way; I invite to Allah." (12:108)

- شَيْء (shay') → thing / something / anything — appears ~519 times
  Ayah: "إِنَّمَا أَمْرُهُ إِذَا أَرَادَ شَيْئًا أَن يَقُولَ لَهُ كُن فَيَكُونُ" — "His command is only that when He intends a thing, He says to it: Be! And it is." (36:82)

- قَلْب (qalb) → heart (seat of understanding and emotion) — appears ~132 times
  Ayah: "أَفَلَا يَتَدَبَّرُونَ الْقُرْآنَ أَمْ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبٍ أَقْفَالُهَا" — "Then do they not reflect upon the Quran, or are there locks upon their hearts?" (47:24)

## Root Connection

- **حَقّ** (root: ح-ق-ق, h-q-q) → تَحْقِيق (verification/realization), حَقِيقَة (truth/reality), مُحَقَّق (verified). الْحَقّ is one of Allah's names — The Truth/The Reality.
- **نَفْس** (root: ن-ف-س, n-f-s) → تَنَفَّسَ (to breathe), نَفَس (breath), نَفَّاسَة (jealousy). In the Quran نَفْس carries enormous weight — it is the individual soul held accountable before God.
- **قَلْب** (root: ق-ل-ب, q-l-b) → تَقَلُّب (turning/fluctuation), مُتَقَلِّب (wavering). The root implies turning and reversing — the قَلْب is the part of you that "turns" toward or away from God.
- **شَيْء** (root: ش-ي-أ, sh-y-') is the most general noun in Arabic — "a thing." Its philosophical weight in the Quran is immense: اللَّهُ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ (Allah has power over all things).

## Practice

1. نَفْس appears in both Lesson 4 (كُلُّ نَفْسٍ ذَائِقَةُ الْمَوْتِ) and here. What different dimensions of the soul do these two verses illuminate?
2. The word سَبِيل often appears with فِي — "فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّه" (in the way/cause of Allah). What range of actions does this phrase cover in the Quran?
3. In (36:82), شَيْء appears in the context of divine creation. How does its use as "a thing" emphasize God's absolute creative power?

## Reflection

> "أَلَا بِذِكْرِ اللَّهِ تَطْمَئِنُّ الْقُلُوبُ"
> "Verily, in the remembrance of Allah do hearts find rest." (13:28)

The plural قُلُوبُ (hearts) is used here — hearts in general, every kind of heart — making this a universal statement. The قَلْب seeks what only the حَقّ (Truth) can provide.
