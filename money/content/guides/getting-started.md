# Getting Started with Aman Money

## Your First Week

### Day 1: Tell Aman Money about yourself
- Your monthly income (after tax)
- Your biggest recurring expenses (rent, car payment, subscriptions)
- One financial goal (save for emergency fund, pay off debt, save for a trip)

### Day 2-7: Track everything you spend
Just message the bot whenever you spend money:
- "Spent 12 on lunch"
- "Grab to work 8.50"
- "Groceries 85"
- "Netflix subscription 45"

That's it. No app to open. No receipt to scan. Just text.

### End of Week 1: Ask for a summary
Say "summary" and see where your money went. Most people are surprised.

## Tips for Success
- Track immediately — don't wait until evening to remember
- Don't stress about categories — the bot categorizes for you
- Round up — RM 12.30 → "13 on food" is fine
- Include everything — even RM 2 for parking
- Set a budget after 2 weeks of tracking — you need real data first

## Common Phrases the Bot Understands
- "Spent [amount] on [thing]" — logs an expense
- "Received [amount]" or "Salary [amount]" — logs income
- "Budget" — set up or view your budget
- "Save for [goal]" — create a savings goal
- "Summary" or "How am I doing?" — see your financial overview
- "Report" — detailed monthly report
- "I pay [amount] monthly for [thing]" — log recurring expense
