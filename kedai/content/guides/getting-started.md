# Getting Started with Aman Kedai

## Day 1: Set Up Your Menu

Tell the bot your menu items:
```
Menu:
Nasi Lemak - RM 7
Nasi Lemak Special - RM 10
Nasi Goreng - RM 8
Roti Canai - RM 2.50
Teh Tarik - RM 3.50
Kopi O - RM 2.50
Air Kosong - RM 1
```

Or just start logging orders — the bot learns your menu as you go!

## Day 2-7: Log Orders As They Come

Just message naturally:
- "Ali ordered 2 nasi lemak and 1 teh tarik"
- "Walk-in: 1 roti canai, 1 teh tarik, bungkus"
- "Makcik Siti the usual" (works once you've logged her orders a few times)
- "Table 3: 3 nasi goreng, 2 milo ais"

The bot confirms immediately with the total.

## End of Day: Get Your Summary

Say "how was today" and see:
- Total orders and revenue
- Best-selling items
- New vs regular customers

## Tips
- Log EVERY order — even small ones. The data adds up.
- Mark sold-out items immediately — prevents disappointed customers
- Name your regulars — "Ali's usual" saves time during rush hour
- Check the daily summary every night — you'll spot patterns within a week
- Add daily specials in the morning — the bot tracks how they perform

## Common Phrases
| Say this | What happens |
|----------|-------------|
| "[Name] ordered [items]" | Logs an order with customer name |
| "Walk-in: [items]" | Logs anonymous order |
| "[Name]'s usual" | Recalls regular's favorite order |
| "Sold out: [item]" | Marks item unavailable |
| "How was today?" | Daily sales summary |
| "Add item: [name] RM [price]" | Adds to menu |
| "Who is [name]?" | Shows customer profile and history |
| "Weekly report" | Shows week's performance |
