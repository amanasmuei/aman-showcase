# Aman Kedai — Business Types

Aman Kedai adapts to any small business. Here's how it works for each type.

## 🍛 Kedai Makan (Restaurant)

**Setup:** Tell the bot "I run a kedai makan"

**Example interaction:**
```
Owner: Ali ordered 2 nasi lemak, 1 teh tarik
Bot:   Order #012 — Ali (dine-in)
       2x Nasi Lemak    RM 14.00
       1x Teh Tarik      RM  3.50
       Total: RM 17.50
```

**What it tracks:** Menu items, orders, customer favorites, daily sales, sold-out items

---
