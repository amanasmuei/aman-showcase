# Lesson 3: Numbers 1-20

## Words
- Satu → 1 (one)
- Dua → 2 (two)
- Tiga → 3 (three)
- Empat → 4 (four)
- Lima → 5 (five)
- Enam → 6 (six)
- Tujuh → 7 (seven)
- Lapan → 8 (eight)

## Extra (for reference)
- Sembilan → 9, Sepuluh → 10
- Sebelas → 11, Dua belas → 12 (pattern: number + belas for 11-19)
- Dua puluh → 20 (pattern: number + puluh for tens)

## Pattern Note
Malay numbers are very regular! 11 = "se-belas" (one-teen), 12 = "dua belas" (two-teen). 20 = "dua puluh" (two-tens). Once you know 1-10, you know 1-99!

## Practice
1. What is "tiga" in English?
2. How do you say "15" in Malay?
3. What comes after "tujuh"?
4. How do you say your age in Malay? (Hint: "Umur saya ___ tahun")
