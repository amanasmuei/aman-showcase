# Lesson 9: Directions

## Words
- Kiri → Left
- Kanan → Right
- Depan → Front/ahead
- Belakang → Behind
- Dekat → Near
- Jauh → Far
- Di mana? → Where?
- Jalan → Road/street/walk

## Grammar Note
"Di" = at/in (location). "Ke" = to (direction/movement). This is a common confusion!
- "Saya di sekolah" = "I am AT school" (I'm there now)
- "Saya ke sekolah" = "I'm going TO school" (I'm moving there)

## Practice
1. How do you ask "Where is the restaurant?" → "Di mana restoran?"
2. Someone says "Belok kiri" — what does it mean?
3. Is the mall near or far? "Pusat beli-belah itu ___" (dekat/jauh)
4. Give directions: "Go straight, turn right" → "Jalan terus, belok kanan"

## Conversation
> You're lost and need to find the nearest train station (stesen kereta api). Ask a friendly stranger for directions. They guide you using left, right, near, and far.
