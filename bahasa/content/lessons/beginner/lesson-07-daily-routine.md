# Lesson 7: Daily Routine

## Words
- Bangun → Wake up
- Mandi → Shower/bathe
- Tidur → Sleep
- Pergi → Go
- Balik → Return/go home
- Kerja → Work
- Pagi → Morning
- Malam → Night

## Time Words
- Pagi → morning (6am-12pm)
- Tengah hari → noon/midday
- Petang → afternoon/evening (2pm-7pm)
- Malam → night (7pm onwards)

## Pattern
Daily routine sentences: "Saya [verb] [time]"
- "Saya bangun pagi" = "I wake up in the morning"
- "Saya tidur malam" = "I sleep at night"
- "Saya pergi kerja" = "I go to work"

## Practice
1. How do you say "I wake up in the morning"?
2. What does "Saya balik malam" mean?
3. Describe your morning routine in 3 sentences using Malay.
4. What's the difference between "pergi" and "balik"?

## Conversation
> Tell your language partner about your typical day, from waking up to going to sleep. Use time words and daily routine vocabulary.
