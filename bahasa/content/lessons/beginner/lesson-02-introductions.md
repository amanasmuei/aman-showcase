# Lesson 2: Introductions

## Words
- Nama saya → My name is
- Saya dari → I'm from
- Saya tinggal di → I live in
- Umur saya → My age is
- Tahun → Year(s)
- Encik → Mr. (formal)
- Cik → Miss/Ms. (formal)
- Siapa nama awak? → What is your name?

## Grammar Note
"Saya" means "I/my". In Malay, "saya" + noun = "my [noun]". Example: "Nama saya Ali" = "My name is Ali". No verb needed!

## Practice
1. How do you say "My name is [your name]" in Malay?
2. How do you ask someone their name?
3. Say "I'm from [your country]" in Malay.
4. Introduce yourself: name, where you're from, and where you live.

## Conversation
> You're at a company event. A colleague walks up and introduces themselves. Introduce yourself back using today's words and greetings from Lesson 1.
