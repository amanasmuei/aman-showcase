# Lesson 1: Greetings

## Words
- Selamat pagi → Good morning
- Selamat petang → Good afternoon
- Selamat malam → Good evening/night
- Apa khabar? → How are you?
- Khabar baik → I'm fine
- Terima kasih → Thank you
- Sama-sama → You're welcome

## Cultural Note
Malaysians often greet with "Dah makan?" (Have you eaten?). It's not a literal question about food — it's like saying "How are you?" Just reply "Dah!" (Already!) or "Belum" (Not yet).

## Practice
1. How do you say "good morning" in Malay?
2. Someone says "Apa khabar?" — how do you reply?
3. How do you say "thank you"?
4. What's the difference between "selamat pagi" and "selamat malam"?
