# Lesson 6: Colors & Objects

## Words
- Merah → Red
- Biru → Blue
- Hijau → Green
- Kuning → Yellow
- Putih → White
- Hitam → Black
- Buku → Book
- Meja → Table

## Grammar Note
In Malay, adjectives come AFTER the noun. This is the opposite of English!
- "Red book" = "Buku merah" (book red)
- "Blue table" = "Meja biru" (table blue)

This is the #1 mistake beginners make. Practice it until it feels natural.

## Practice
1. How do you say "red book" in Malay? (Remember: noun first!)
2. What color is "hijau"?
3. Describe something on your desk using a color: "___ ___" (object + color)
4. Translate: "The white table" → "Meja ___"

## Conversation
> You're shopping for school supplies. Describe what you need using colors and objects. The shopkeeper helps you find them.
