# Aman Bahasa — Core Vocabulary

## Beginner (Lessons 1-10): ~80 words

### Greetings & Social
selamat pagi (good morning), selamat petang (good afternoon), selamat malam (good evening), apa khabar (how are you), khabar baik (I'm fine), terima kasih (thank you), sama-sama (you're welcome), tolong (please/help), maaf (sorry), ya (yes), tidak/tak (no)

### People & Family
saya (I/me/my), awak (you), dia (he/she), kami (we), ibu (mother), ayah (father), adik (younger sibling), abang (older brother), kakak (older sister), anak (child), keluarga (family), kawan (friend), encik (Mr.), cik (Miss)

### Numbers
satu (1), dua (2), tiga (3), empat (4), lima (5), enam (6), tujuh (7), lapan (8), sembilan (9), sepuluh (10), sebelas (11), dua belas (12), dua puluh (20), seratus (100)

### Food & Drinks
nasi (rice), ayam (chicken), ikan (fish), air (water), teh (tea), kopi (coffee), makan (eat), minum (drink), masak (cook), nasi lemak, teh tarik, roti canai

### Colors
merah (red), biru (blue), hijau (green), kuning (yellow), putih (white), hitam (black)

### Daily Life
bangun (wake up), mandi (shower), tidur (sleep), pergi (go), balik (return), kerja (work), buku (book), meja (table)

### Shopping
berapa (how much), ringgit (RM), mahal (expensive), murah (cheap), beli (buy), jual (sell)

### Directions
kiri (left), kanan (right), depan (front), belakang (behind), dekat (near), jauh (far), di mana (where), jalan (road/walk)

### Transport
kereta (car), bas (bus), teksi (taxi), kereta api (train), naik (ride/take), turun (get off), berhenti (stop), stesen (station)

### Time
pagi (morning), tengah hari (noon), petang (afternoon), malam (night), hari ini (today), esok (tomorrow), semalam (yesterday), tahun (year)

### Useful Phrases
saya nak... (I want...), boleh? (can?), di sini (here), di sana (there), ini (this), itu (that), ada (have/there is), tiada (don't have/there isn't)
