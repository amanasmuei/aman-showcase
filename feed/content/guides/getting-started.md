# Getting Started with Aman Feed

## Your First Conversation

### Step 1: Tell it what you care about
```
You: I'm interested in AI, Malaysian startups, and badminton

Aman Feed: Great! I've added these to your feed:
           - AI / Machine Learning (high priority)
           - Malaysian Startups (high priority)
           - Badminton (high priority)

           Want me to suggest related topics you might like?
```

### Step 2: Get your first briefing
```
You: What's new?

Aman Feed: Your briefing:

           1. [AI] OpenAI released GPT-5 with reasoning capabilities...
           2. [Startups] Carsome raises $300M Series E, largest SEA auto-tech round...
           3. [Badminton] Lee Zii Jia advances to All England semis...
           4. [AI] Malaysia's MDEC launches national AI roadmap 2.0...
           5. [Startups] Grab integrates AI chat into driver app...

           Reply with a number to go deeper on any topic.
```

### Step 3: Go deep on what interests you
```
You: Tell me more about #4

Aman Feed: Malaysia's National AI Roadmap 2.0:

           MDEC unveiled an updated AI strategy focusing on...
           [detailed analysis with context, implications, and connections]

           This connects to your interest in AI and Malaysian startups —
           several local AI companies are likely to benefit...
```

## Tips
- Say "briefing" or "what's new" anytime for a personalized update
- Share links and articles — the bot summarizes and remembers them
- Say "follow [topic]" to add new interests
- Say "unfollow [topic]" to remove topics you no longer care about
- Say "my feed" to see and adjust all your followed topics
- The bot learns from your behavior — topics you go deep on get higher priority
