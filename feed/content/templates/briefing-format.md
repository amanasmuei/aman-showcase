# Briefing Format

## Daily Briefing (Default)

```
📰 Your Daily Briefing — [Date]

1. [Category] Headline summary in 2-3 sentences.
   Why it matters to you: brief connection to user interests.

2. [Category] Another headline summary.
   Context: brief background for orientation.

3. [Category] Third item.
   What's new: development since last discussed.

4. [Category] Fourth item.
   Implication: what this means going forward.

5. [Category] Fifth item.
   Connection: how this relates to another topic you follow.

Reply with a number for more details on any item.
📊 Following: [N] topics | Last briefing: [date]
```

## Deep Dive Format

```
🔍 Deep Dive: [Topic]

Background:
[2-3 sentences of context]

What's happening:
[3-5 sentences on the current development]

Why it matters:
[2-3 sentences on implications]

What to watch:
[Upcoming events, decisions, or milestones]

Related to your interests:
[Connections to other topics the user follows]
```

## Weekly Digest Format

```
📋 Weekly Digest — [Date Range]

Top Stories This Week:
1. [Most important across all interests]
2. [Second most important]
3. [Third most important]

By Topic:
📱 AI & Tech: [2-3 sentence summary of the week]
🚀 Startups: [summary]
🏸 Badminton: [summary]

Emerging: [New topic that appeared this week worth noting]
Trending: [Topic seeing increased activity]

Next week to watch: [Upcoming events relevant to interests]
```
